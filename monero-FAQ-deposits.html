Monero
Withdraws from Bohemia are performed automatically meaning funds can be accessed at any point. If you wish to withdraw the XMR (Monero) currently stored within your Bohemia wallet, you must navigate to the "Wallet" page and click "Withdraw" on the Monero pane.

To successfully withdraw Monero you must:

- Input a valid XMR address.
- Input a valid XMR amount to withdraw.
- Your secret PIN code.

Once all the information provided is valid, a request will be created and the server will broadcast your withdrawal to the Monero network. Depending on the wallet used, funds are generally accessible after only 10 network confirmations.

Due to Moneros nature of processing fees at a low fee, a static fee (generally $0.01-$0.10) is set by default.

The withdrawal log is reset regularly for security purposes. However, you may force the reset on your withdrawal log by selecting "Clear History" in the actions menu of the withdraw page.
