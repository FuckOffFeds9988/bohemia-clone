Bitcoin
Bohemia accepts BTC (Bitcoin) as a method of deposit to your Bohemia account wallet. Selecting the "Deposit" option on the Bitcoin pane of the "Wallet" page will present you with two pieces of data:

- A QR Code
- A BTC Address

Assuming that you are familiar with how Bitcoin works, you must send any funds you wish to deposit to the displayed address. For convenience, you can scan the QR code using your mobile device if you are using a mobile-based wallet.

For the deposited funds to be assigned to your Bohemia wallet, a minimum of 3 network confirmations will need to take place. You can keep track of the current amount of confirmations on the deposit page.

IMPORTANT: You can only deposit to the displayed address ONCE. The BTC (Bitcoin) address associated with your wallet will rotate with each deposit. No action on your behalf is required.
