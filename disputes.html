Disputes
At times there may be disagreements between parties whilst partaking and / or concluding a transaction.

For example: A buyer may not receive what they purchased as per advertised and the merchant has failed to amicably rectify the situation. Effectively, a dispute can now been lodged from the buyer. This will launch a dispute and allocate a staff member for the purposes of moderation between both parties until a resolution can be agreed upon. In the event that a solution has not been solidified, the allocated staff member can intervene and end the dispute in favor of the merchant or buyer. Our staff members have been heavily briefed on how they should conduct themselves whilst handling disputes, to always keeping an open mind and to always approach a dispute with a fair attitude and level of understanding for both parties involved when making a final decision..

At any point throughout an orders journey (from waiting to be fulfilled to being finalized), orders are disputable through the orders page. The drop-down will present the option to dispute an order. You will then be asked for the context of the dispute. What made you open the dispute.

NOTE: Its important that you as a merchant (or buyer) attempt to resolve the situation amicably. Merchants have the ability to re-issue orders, or to refund any orders received.

Any messages sent through the active dispute are viewable by both parties (buyer & merchant), but all also by the allocated staff member. Time will be allocated to the dispute as to allow both parties to reach an agreement.

The solution can be either:

- Close dispute, and finalize order (In favor of vendor).
- Close dispute, and refund order (In favor of buyer).

Once closed, a dispute cannot be reinstated, and the decision is final.
