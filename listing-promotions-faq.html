Listing Promotions
Here at Bohemia, we have took the decision to implement numerous features which are not only advantageous when promoting products on the platform, but affordable too.

There are two features to choose from, which consist of the following:

- Stickied Listing
This feature sticks the selected listing to the top of its allocated category as per set when creating and / or editing the listing in question. Potential buyers will see this listing above all others when browsing the respective category, therefore providing first class exposure when advertising your products.

- Highlighted Listing
This feature surrounds your listing tile with an eye catching yellow border within the category allocated when creating and / or editing the listing in question. Potential buyers will struggle to miss your listing when browsing the respective category, therefore generating further exposure when advertising your products.

To view, check prices and select your desired promotion duration, as well as view statistics relating to current and previous promotions youve enrolled in, head to the "Listing Promotions" page via the "Merchant Shortcuts" dropdown located along the top toolbar of the platform, or via the "Merchant CP".

NOTICE: Please ensure that your wallet balance is sufficient before commiting to any of the features mentioned above. Failure to do so will result in error.
