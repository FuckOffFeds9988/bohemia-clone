Receiving Merchant Alerts
Bohemia has implemented a useful alert tool for merchants to be able to maximize their potential reach while increasing their total possible revenue. This feature (better known as Merchant Alerts), broadcasts messages en-masse to all loyal customers for which have purchased from a particular merchant 5 or more times.

Once the threshold has been met, you (the buyer) will be assigned to the merchants alert list. This merchant will then have the ability to alert you of any product additions, restocks and discount codes directly to your inbox. In case you wish to not be included in a particular merchants alert list, you can simply click the "Unsubscribe" button which can be located on the merchants profile page for which you are subscribed.
