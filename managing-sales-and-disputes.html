Managing Sales & Disputes
Merchants can be kept up-to-date with any sales, or disputes through the appropriate sales tab on the navigation bar. By visiting this page, you will see all your processing, dispatched, finalized and cancelled orders, alongside any orders which have been disputed by the buyer.

Bohemia adopts a smart system to keep the merchant aware of any changes, by introducing a counter-until-check whereby you will receive the latest updates in regards to any new or finalized sales since the last time you checked.

Disputes can also be tracked, and dealt with using the appropriate dispute section of the sales page.

You can also keep track of any responses, or updates to any disputes by checking your notifications center.
