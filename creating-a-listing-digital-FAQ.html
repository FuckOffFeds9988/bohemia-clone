Creating A Listing (Digital)
Bohemia supports selling digital products, ie: anything which does not have to be physically shipped. This can be anything from methods, to accounts.

The option to create a digital listing can be found when hovering over the "Create Listing" option on the navigation bar. Upon selecting it, you will be given a chance to fill in the information of the product/service which you wish to provide.

Merchants also have the ability to automatically dispatch goods to buyers using the autoship feature built into Bohemia. Data can be inputted into auto-shipment box, while the either the option for concurrent shipment (all contents at once, ie: a method) or consecutive shipment (individual lines, ie: a list of accounts).
