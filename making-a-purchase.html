Making A Purchase
Bohemia employs a smart cart system which gives the buyer full flexibility in customizing their quantity, shipping & payment methods on the fly.

Both BTC (Bitcoin) and XMR (Monero) are accepted here at Bohemia. Its important to ensure that your account is topped up with the amount required to fulfill any order(s) you may wish to place. Certain merchants may only accept BTC while some may only accept XMR. Be sure to check which coins are accepted on any products of interest prior to making a deposit.

Physical products will generally have a field underneath which allows you to submit details such as your home address. While there is a PGP option built into the market, we highly suggest disabling it, and instead using your own PGP key to encrypt the sensitive information you must hand to the vendor.

If you happen to have a coupon code giving you a certain discount on a product, you may redeem it at checkout in the appropriate field. The necessary amount will be automatically deducted upon entry of code.

IMPORTANT: Changing a quantity, shopping method, adding coupon code or even adding a PGP encrypted message to the order must result in pressing the "Update" button, or your progress will be lost. This is extremely important. Any alteration must be followed by the Update button as to make the changes persist.
