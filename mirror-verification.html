Mirror Verification
There are several different ways to ensure that the mirror which you are currently visiting is in fact a genuine mirror operated by Bohemia.

Firstly, you can input "/mirrors.txt" after .onion of the URL currently displayed within your address bar. Our official PGP key can be located via trusted sources such as our official sections on DarkEye and Raptor, or by visiting "/pgp.txt" the same way as per mentioned above when viewing "/mirrors.txt".

If the PGP message cannot be decrypted using our public official PGP public key, and / or if the mirror you are currently accessing the platform from is not visible within the encrypted message, we suggest ending your session immediately.

Another way of validating the legitimacy of a mirror, is by visiting any of the partner websites for which we are affiliated:

- DarkEye (darkeyeb643f2syd[dot]onion)

If the 26 characters presented within the captcha image does not match that of the mirror currently being visited, we suggest ending your session immediately and seeking a mirror from one of the aforementioned trusted sources.
